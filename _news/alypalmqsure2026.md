[Aly Palmquist](/people/alypalmq) will present her summer research at the [2026 SURE Student Showcase](https://sure.engin.umich.edu/sure-showcase/) on **Wednesday, July 29**, from **2:00 to 4:00 p.m.**, in the **Duderstadt Gallery and the Duderstadt Center Atrium**.

This summer, Aly has built an AI-Driven Researcher Profiling and Collaboration Engine that collects and processes technical publications and grants, then applies natural language processing to turn them into structured databases that make it possible to surface research expertise and identify promising collaborations.

Stop by the Duderstadt to see her poster, and congratulations on a great summer, Aly!
