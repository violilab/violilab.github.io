[Angela Violi](/people/avioli) presented at the [2025 Americal Society for Engineering Education Research (ASEE) conference](https://web.archive.org/web/20251005232154/https://cefrc.princeton.edu/combustion-summer-school).
Her paper, titled ["Broadening Participation and Building Resilience,"](https://web.archive.org/web/20251005232717/https://nemo.asee.org/public/conferences/365/registration/view_session?session_id=20291) discusses the persistent underrepresentation of women in engineering (particularly in Mechanical Engineering) by examining enrollment and employment trends, identifying structural and cultural barriers to participation, and highlighting strategies for creating more inclusive academic and professional environments.
