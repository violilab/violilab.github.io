Five papers from the Violi Lab were accepted to the [41st International Symposium on Combustion](https://www.combustionsymposia.org/event/41stisoc/summary) in Kyoto, Japan, four of which were published in the 2026 [Proceedings of the Combustion Institute](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/proceedings-of-the-combustion-institute).

**Published in the Proceedings:**

1. "Mapping Kinetic Bottlenecks in Soot Precursor Growth via Graph Analysis of Stochastic Trajectories":
Matt Raymond, Paolo Elvati, Angela Violi

2. "The effect of probe-induced cooling on PAC growth and characteristics in a premixed ethylene/air flame":
Luke Di Liddo, Francisco Cepeda, Jacob Saldinger, Paolo Elvati, Angela Violi, Seth B. Dworkin

3. "Uncertainty-Aware Smoke-Point Screening of SAF-Rich Aviation Fuels via Optimization and Bayesian Inference":
Mohammed I. Radaideh, Majdi I. Radaideh, Angela Violi

4. "Experimental and theoretical investigation of carbazole formation pathways and soot reduction during ammonia-hydrocarbon co-combustion":
Qi Wang, Guangxian Xu, Paolo Elvati, Qiang Xu, Jiwen Guan, Angela Violi, Zhandong Wang, S. Mani Sarathy

**Also presented at the symposium:**

{:start="5"}
5. "Stochastic Modeling of PAH Growth along Diffusion Flame Streamlines":
Erin McCaughey, Paolo Elvati, Angela Violi, J. Houston Miller
